Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for items?
Simply type the items you want to buy in the search bar, separated by commas. For example: "milk, eggs, bread". Click the Search button and we'll show you prices from stores near you.
Which stores do you compare?
We currently compare prices from major grocery stores including Walmart, Costco, and T&T Supermarket. We're constantly adding more stores to give you better options. So stay tuned!
How do you calculate the total price?
We add up the individual prices of all items in your search from each store, showing you the total cost and sorting stores from lowest to highest price.
Are the prices accurate?
We update our prices regularly to ensure accuracy. However, prices may vary by location and can change. We recommend confirming prices in-store before making your purchase.
What if an item is not available?
If an item you search for is not in our database, we'll show you a notification letting you know which items couldn't be found. You can continue with the available items.
How is distance calculated?
We use your location to calculate the approximate distance to each store, and display the Supermarkets within 5 km. You can allow location access for more accurate results, or we'll use a default location.
Is Kiwi Kart free to use?
Yes! Kiwi Kart is completely free. Our goal is to help you save money on your grocery shopping.
